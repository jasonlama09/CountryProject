Where is France?

France is a country in western Europe. It shares borders with countries like Spain,
Germany, and Italy, and it has coastlines on the Atlantic Ocean and the Mediterranean Sea.


























Northern France

Lille

A busy city with old brick buildings and lots of students.

Normandy

A coastal area known for D-Day beaches and pretty countryside.

Amiens

A calm city famous for its giant cathedral and canals.

Western France

Brittany (Bretagne)

A place with rocky coasts, lighthouses, and strong Celtic traditions.

Nantes

A lively city with cool art, history, and a big mechanical elephant.

La Rochelle

A sunny port city with old towers and sailboats everywhere.

Southern France

Provence

A place with lavender fields, old villages, and super good food.

Nice

A bright beach city on the Mediterranean with clear blue water.

Marseille

A busy port city with tons of culture and great seafood.

Eastern France

Alsace

A place with half-timbered houses that feels a little German and French at the same time.

Strasbourg

A beautiful city with a huge cathedral and the European Parliament

The Alps

Massive mountains perfect for skiing, hiking, and awesome views.

Central France

Paris

The famous capital city with the Eiffel Tower and tons of museums.

Orleans

A historic city known for Joan of Arc and its pretty river views.

Bourges

A quiet town with a huge cathedral and medieval streets.
