Fun Facts

  1. 1.France is the most visited country in the world. Millions of tourists flock to Paris, the French Riviera, and historic regions each year.
  2. 2.The Eiffel Tower wasn’t supposed to be permanent. Built in 1889, it was almost torn down after 20 years.
  3. 3.France gave the world the metric system. It was introduced during the French Revolution to standardize measurements.
  4. 4.French is spoken on 5 continents. It’s an official language in 29 countries, not just in Europe
  5. 5.France has diverse landscapes. From the Alps and Pyrenees mountains to Mediterranean beaches and lush countryside.
  6. 6.France invented the hot air balloon. The first successful flight was in 1783.
  7. 7.The Louvre is the largest art museum in the world. It houses thousands of masterpieces, including the Mona Lisa.
