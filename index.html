Louis Vuitton

Louis Vuitton is a really fancy brand from France.
They make nice bags and clothes that a lot of people love because they’re made super well and last a long time.Their bags usually have the letters L and V on them, so they’re easy to spot.

Dior

Dior is a famous brand from France that makes beautiful dresses, perfumes, and makeup. People like Dior because everything they make feels fancy and elegant, like something you would wear to a special event.

CELINE

Celine is a French brand that makes simple but very stylish clothes and bags. The designs look clean and modern, and a lot of people think it looks really cool without being too flashy.

Yves Saint Laurent (YSL)

Yves Saint Laurent usually called (YSL) is a brand known for bold, stylish clothes and makeup. Their designs feel energetic and fashionable. Their logo has the letters Y, S, and L stacked together, and it’s pretty iconic.
