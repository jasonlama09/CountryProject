French Cuisine

France is a country in western Europe. It shares borders with countries like Spain,
Germany, and Italy, and it has coastlines on the Atlantic Ocean and the Mediterranean Sea.


































Breakfast

Croissant

A light, buttery pastry that’s flaky and perfect for a classic French morning.

Pain au Chocolat

A croissant-like pastry filled with rich chocolate for a sweet start.

French Baguette with Jam

Fresh, crusty bread served with butter and fruit jam for a simple morning treat.

Lunch

Niçoise Salad

A fresh salad made with tuna, eggs, olives, and crisp vegetables.

Quiche Lorraine

A savory tart with eggs, cheese, and bacon, perfect for midday meals.

Croque-Monsieur

A toasted ham-and-cheese sandwich topped with melted cheese.

Dinner

Boeuf Bourguignon

A rich beef stew slow-cooked with red wine, carrots, onions, and herbs.

Coq au Vin

Chicken braised in red wine with mushrooms and onions.

Ratatouille

A slow-cooked vegetable medley of zucchini, peppers, and tomatoes.

Appetizer

Gougères

Light, airy cheese puffs made from choux dough, served warm before a meal.

Bruschetta-style Tartines

Toasted bread topped with tomatoes, herbs, or cheese.

Baked Camembert

Soft cheese baked until melty, served with bread for dipping.
